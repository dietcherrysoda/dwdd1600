Fashion & Style

Finding Your Own Personal Style !

Finding your personal style isn’t about following every trend — it’s about discovering what makes you feel confident, comfortable, and true to who you are. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you along the way:

  1. Start With What You Love
  2. Look Inside Your Closet
  3. Experiment
  4. Shop With Intention
  5. Seek Visual Inspiration
  6. Dress For Your Lifestyle
  7. Embrace Your Style Evolution

Using a platform like Pinterest, is a great way to get inspiration and start your style journey!

Style Tips While Also Being Modesty

  • Balance Fit & Coverage – Choose pieces that are loose but still flattering.
  • Layer Creatively – Add coverage with turtlenecks, cardigans, or button-downs.
  • Experiment with Texture & Color – Mix fabrics and don’t shy away from prints or bold tones.
  • Add Shape with Belts – Define your waist with belts over dresses or tunics.
  • Use Statement Accessories – Elevate simple outfits with scarves, jewelry, or bags.
  • Go Long with Style – Choose maxi skirts or dresses with fun details like pleats or tiers.

Shopping Sustainably

How to Shop Sustainably
You can start by thrifting, shopping seconq adhand online, or supporting small and ethical brands that use eco-friendly materials.
Being Mindful
Means making mindful choices that benefit both the planet and the people behind our clothes. Buying fewer, better-quality pieces and taking care of what you own can make a big difference. Look for timeless styles, learn to read fabric labels, and try swapping or borrowing instead of always buying new.
Make Your Own Wardrobe
You can even make your own, by sewing, crocheting, or even knitting your own garments! Sustainable fashion is all about being intentional — not perfect — and making choices that align with your values and personal style.

One way that I like to shop sustainably is by shopping on Depop, a reselling app that currates to your own personal style!
