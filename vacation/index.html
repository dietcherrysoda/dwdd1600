Welcome to Paradise

Discover Juayúa

A tropical hidden gem that is located in the heart of El Salvador. Nestled in the mountains and rich in culture, Juayúa invites you to slow down, explore, and reconnect.

Our Story

Angel Beach Travels began with a simple vision: to share the hidden beauty of Juayúa, a quiet mountain town along El Salvador's iconic Ruta de Las Flores. As a woman-founded, locally-inspired travel agency, we focus on intentional, peaceful escapes for those looking to reconnect with nature, culture, and themselves.

About Juayúa

Known for its lush landscapes, weekend food festivals, and artisan markets, Juayúa offers a unique blend of nature, flavor, and tradition. Whether you're a foodie, adventurer, or seeker of peace, Juayúa promises an unforgettable experience.

Popular Destinations

  • Los Chorros de la Calera Waterfalls: Take a guided hike to this hidden paradise nestled in the mountains and surrounded by dense greenery.
  • Coffee Plantations This region is famous for its high-quality coffee. Visit a local finca (farm), walk among the coffee trees, learn the process from bean to cup, and enjoy a freshly brewed coffee with a view.
  • Old Railway Station A historical gem, the old train station of Juayúa offers a glimpse into the town's past. It's now a quaint gathering place and occasional site for pop-up art and food events.
  • La Mirador de la Ruta For panoramic mountain views and unforgettable sunsets, head to this scenic viewpoint just outside town. It's a peaceful spot to reflect, relax, and take in the beauty of western El Salvador.
