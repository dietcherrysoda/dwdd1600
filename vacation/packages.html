Vacation Get-Away Packages

La Naturaleza Package

Escape into serenity with this 4-day nature retreat in the vibrant landscape of the Ruta de las Flores. Wake up to misty mountain air, embark on guided hikes through coffee plantations and waterfalls like Los Chorros de la Calera, and unwind in a stylish mountain cabin. The spacious, plant-filled retreat invites you to sip coffee on the terrace and reconnect with both nature and yourself.

Destination: Juayúa, El Salvador

Hotel: Vista Montaña Cabin

Rental Car: Toyota RAV4 from Alamo Rent a Car

Cost: $920 USD per person

La Cultura Package

For city lovers this 5-day getaway takes you through Juayúa, Ataco, and Apaneca—perfect for creative minds and culture enthusiasts. Enjoy artisan markets, murals, and traditional food.

Destination: Juayúa + Ataco + Apaneca

Hotel: Hotel Mamapan

Rental Car: Kia Sportage from Hertz

Cost: $1,100 USD per person

Café Con Pan Package

A 3-day retreat for peace, creativity, and nature. Enjoy your morning coffee with mountain views, a coffee farm tour, and luxury in a modern, forested Airbnb.

Destination: Juayúa & Surrounding Mountains

Stay: Aurora - Volcano Cabin (Airbnb)

Rental Car: Nissan X-Trail from Enterprise

Cost: $975 USD per person
